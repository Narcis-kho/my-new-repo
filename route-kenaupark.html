Kenaupark – Easy 2 km city run

Right in the heart of Haarlem, Kenaupark offers a calm and compact space for short runs.

This 2 km loop circles the elegant park with its tree-lined paths and historic charm. Flat and safe—ideal if you’re just starting. Near Haarlem station; easy to reach and perfect for a morning or evening jog.

Starting point: Duintlustweg, near Haarlem Station. Just steps from Haarlem’s central train station, making it super accessible.

Nearby Activities

Nearby Activities:
Explore Haarlem Old Town and the Grote Markt. Take a canal tour or visit Teylers Museum.
