Spaarne River – 5 km riverside path

Follow the Spaarne River for a run filled with iconic Haarlem scenery and calming water views.

This 5 km route runs alongside the river, passing historic houses and boats. Flat and open—great for steady pacing while enjoying a scenic environment. The lively atmosphere makes it easy to stay motivated, whether you’re training or relaxing.

Starting point: Near Mooie Nel Bridge, Spaarne 16.

Nearby Activities

Nearby Activities: Stroll through Centrum Haarlem; visit Teylers Museum.
