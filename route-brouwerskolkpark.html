Brouwerskolkpark – Scenic nature loop

Step into Brouwerskolkpark, a quiet park in Overveen where forest paths and a sparkling lake create a refreshing running experience.

This 2–3 km loop takes you past the lake, over small bridges, and through forest trails. The viewing platform offers a rewarding pause with peaceful views across the water.

Starting point: Brouwerskolkweg entrance, Overveen. Accessible by foot from Overveen station.

Nearby Activities

Nearby Activities: Relax at the Brouwerskolkpark pond.
